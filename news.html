News & Updates

Stay informed with the latest announcements and updates from FUO SUG

Announcement January 25, 2025

New Student Orientation Program 2025

The Student Union Government is pleased to announce the commencement of the New Student Orientation Program for the 2024/2025 academic session. All newly admitted students are required to participate in this comprehensive program designed to familiarize them with university life, academic expectations, and available resources.

The orientation will cover important topics including academic regulations, student services, campus facilities, and student rights and responsibilities. Special sessions will be dedicated to introducing students to various clubs, societies, and extracurricular activities available on campus.

Public Relations Officer
Academic January 20, 2025

Academic Excellence Awards Ceremony

FUO SUG proudly announces the upcoming Academic Excellence Awards Ceremony to celebrate outstanding academic achievements of our students. The ceremony will recognize students who have demonstrated exceptional performance in their studies during the previous academic session.

Awards will be presented in various categories including Best Graduating Student, Most Improved Student, and Faculty-specific excellence awards. The event will also feature keynote speeches from distinguished alumni and faculty members.

Academic Affairs Director
Student Welfare January 15, 2025

Enhanced Student Welfare Initiative

The Student Union Government has launched a comprehensive student welfare initiative aimed at improving the overall campus experience for all FUUO students. This initiative includes improvements to hostel facilities, dining services, and recreational amenities.

Key improvements include extended library hours, upgraded internet connectivity in hostels, new recreational facilities, and enhanced medical services at the university health center. The SUG is committed to ensuring that every student has access to quality support services.

Welfare Director
Announcement January 10, 2025

SUG Election Guidelines Released

The Electoral Committee has released the official guidelines for the upcoming Student Union Government elections. All interested candidates are encouraged to review the requirements and procedures for nomination and campaigning.

The election process will be conducted transparently with multiple polling stations across campus. Students are reminded of their civic responsibility to participate in the democratic process by voting for their preferred candidates.

Electoral Committee
