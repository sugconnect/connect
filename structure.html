Our Leadership

The Federal University Otuoke Student Union Government is structured to ensure effective representation and service delivery to all students. Our leadership team is committed to transparency, accountability, and student welfare.

Executive Council

Zolo Charles

SUG President

The President serves as the chief executive officer of the Student Union Government, representing students' interests and overseeing all SUG activities.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Overall leadership of SUG
  • Student representation to university management
  • Policy formulation and implementation
  • Chairing executive meetings

Jane Smith

SUG Vice President

The Vice President assists the President and takes charge in their absence, focusing on internal affairs and student welfare programs.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Assisting the President
  • Coordinating internal affairs
  • Student welfare oversight
  • Acting President when necessary

Michael Johnson

SUG Secretary General

The Secretary General manages all administrative functions, maintains records, and ensures proper documentation of SUG activities.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Administrative management
  • Record keeping and documentation
  • Meeting coordination
  • Official correspondence

Sarah Williams

SUG Treasurer

The Treasurer manages all financial affairs of the Student Union Government, ensuring transparency and accountability in financial matters.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Financial management
  • Budget preparation and monitoring
  • Financial reporting
  • Audit coordination
